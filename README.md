# satoshipierogi 👋
## Overview
This repository serves as the public hub for my work under the Satoshi Pierogi creator brand. It combines my interests across crypto, AI, and productivity into one space. The goal is to make it easier for others to explore my projects, research, and experiments while creating a transparent portfolio of what I’m building.

Think of this repo as a living notebook:

* Crypto/DeFi analysis & research frameworks
* AI workflows, prompts, and automation experiments
* Content systems, templates, and process designs
* Portfolio of projects, experiments, and prototypes
---
## Why This Exists
The idea behind satoshipierogi is to bridge three worlds:

1. Crypto & DeFi – tokenomics analysis, protocol research, zk-tech exploration
2. AI & Automation – workflows, multi-model stacks, agentic systems
3. Content & Productivity – systems for scaling writing, research, and creativity

By putting these together, the repo aims to show how technical experimentation, research, and systems design can come together into a single creative toolkit.
